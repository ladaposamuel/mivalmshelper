## Detailed Description

Miva LMS Study Helper is a browser extension designed specifically for Miva University students to track and manage their learning progress.

**Key Features:**

**Progress Tracking**
Automatically tracks which course activities you've visited and completed. See your progress at a glance with visual indicators directly on the course page.

**Visual Completion Indicators**
Green checkmarks appear next to completed activities, making it easy to see what you've done and what's left. Progress bars show your completion percentage for each course.

**Study List**
Bookmark your current semester courses to a personal study list for quick access. Manage your priorities and stay focused on what matters.

**Detailed Progress View**
Open the progress modal to see a breakdown of your completion by week and section. Identify gaps in your learning and plan your study sessions effectively.

**Data Control**
Your data stays on your device. Export your progress data anytime as a backup, import it on another device, or clear it when you're done with a semester.

**Privacy First**
This extension stores all data locally on your device. No data is ever sent to external servers. No tracking, no analytics, no third-party services.

---

**How to Use:**

1. Install the extension and navigate to Miva LMS
2. Browse your courses - the extension automatically tracks your progress
3. Click "Add to Study List" on courses you want to prioritize
4. Click the extension icon to see your study list and overall progress
5. Use the Settings page to manage your data and view statistics

## Permissions Justification

When submitting, you may need to justify permissions:

| Permission | Justification |
|------------|---------------|
| storage | Required to save course progress data locally on the user's device |
| activeTab | Required to read course and activity information from the current Miva LMS page |
| tabs | Required to open extension views (settings page) in new tabs |
| Host: lms.miva.university | The extension only operates on Miva University's LMS - this is the only domain it needs access to |
